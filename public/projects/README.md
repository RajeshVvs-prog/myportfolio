## Recommended Specifications

- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Dimensions**: 800x600px (4:3 aspect ratio)
- **File Size**: Under 500KB for optimal loading
- **Quality**: High resolution, clear screenshots of your projects

## Tips

- Use actual screenshots of your deployed projects
- Ensure good lighting and contrast
- Crop to show the most important features
- Optimize images before uploading (use tools like TinyPNG)

Once you add your images here, they will automatically appear in your portfolio!
